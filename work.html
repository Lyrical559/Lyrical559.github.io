This is about myWork

Back when i was growing up i remember going through countless jobs from staffing services some work great like working in commodities out in Selma was a blast learning to drive bulldozers and forklifts and bobcats to loading up big rigs with grain and learning how to unload the grains from the train so much I learned at that job.Then i have had terrible jobs like irrigation where you work swing shifts all the time my boss was there for 3 years and still not hired on 12 hour shifts every day makes it very hard when you go to lunch and you come back to your coworker not knowing what he is even doing. At the moment i work in retail not my first choice but its a great job and it allows me time for going to college.
